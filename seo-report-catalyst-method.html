Recommended Actions — Prioritised

Priority 1 — Critical Fixes (block ranking or kill conversions)

  • 1
    Homepage title tag: Cut from 72 to ≤60 chars. New title: "Lead Generation for Local Businesses | The Catalyst Method". This is the single highest-impact SEO change on the site — truncated titles in SERPs reduce click-through rate significantly.
  • 2
    Add LocalBusiness JSON-LD to homepage: Without this schema, Google can't confidently include the site in local pack results. Add name, url, email, areaServed: "United Kingdom", sameAs links.
  • 3
    Add social proof to homepage: Add 2–3 client testimonials with real outcomes (e.g. "We went from 0 to 12 enquiries per month in 8 weeks — [Client Name], plumber"). Zero social proof on a homepage is a conversion killer.
  • 4
    Add soft CTA + testimonials to /signup: Visitors who aren't ready to buy need a downgrade path. Add "Not ready? Get your free website audit →" below the plan cards. Also add 2 short client quotes above the plans.
  • 5
    Trim /about meta description to ≤160 chars: Currently 205 chars — Google truncates it. The cut-off version will end mid-sentence, looking broken in SERPs.
  • 6
    Trim /faq meta description to ≤160 chars: Currently 190 chars — same issue.
  • 7
    Add E-E-A-T to /about: Add founder name, role, and credentials. Google's Helpful Content guidelines specifically reward pages where real expertise is demonstrated. Abstract "we" language scores poorly for YMYL (financial/business advice) topics.
